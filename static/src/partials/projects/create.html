Sense-it missions are collaborative projects in which people collect data together. To collect data, the Android Sense-it app is used.

As the mission owner, you will be able to decide what sensors are used to collect data. The available sensors are: Accelerometer, Sound, Magnetic field, Gyroscope, Orientation, Pressure, Ambient temperature, and Relative humidity. (Please note that not all mobile devices has all these sensors.)

Spot-it missions are collaborative projects in which people collect pictures together about a specific topic.

As the mission owner, you will be able to decide the topic of the pictures. Uploaded pictures can be voted, and you can also delete inappropriate pictures if necessary.

Win-it missions are competitive projects in which participante propose answers to a question or challenge.

As the mission owner, you will:

  • Determine the challenge.
  • Specify the format of the ideas, by selecting a number of fields to be completed by the participants.
  • Control the stage of the mission: proposal, voting, and winner announcement.
  • Select the winner.

You are allowed to offer prizes for your missions! Check some examples in the front page of this site.
